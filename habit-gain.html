Problems

Many individuals like me struggle to stay motivated or find it difficult to stick to their goals without a sense of progress or accountability when trying to build new habits. Existing habit tracking apps mostly focus on recording habits without emphasizing the positive gains and progress achieved over time.

Product explained

Introducing "HabitGain" - the ultimate habit tracker app designed to empower you on your journey of self-improvement. HabitGain is not just your regular habit tracker; it goes beyond tracking and focuses on showcasing the gains you've achieved throughout your habit-building process. With HabitGain, you can effortlessly monitor your daily habits, whether it's exercising, meditating, reading, or any other goal you set for yourself. The app provides a user-friendly interface that allows you to create and customize your habits, set specific targets, and track your progress with ease.

Conclusion

To sum up, "HabitGain" aims to change habit tracking by addressing the motivational challenges individuals often face. The app aims to creates an engaging experience, enhance motivation, accountability, and personal growth by integrating positive gains and progress into design.
