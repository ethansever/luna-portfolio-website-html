Problems and Solutions

Zanova as a brand has a minimalistic approach to their box designs with flat design single line arts. But their user interface does not mirror their approach. Their websites contains too many same product photos with extensive texts which makes user scroll through without paying attention. There was no color palette in the UI, the colors used were white black and cream.

Home Page: Cluttered user interface with too much text and oversize images.
Catalog Page: Inconsistent cards among products

Solution

Instead of extreme minimalism, to add a playful and nature and to highlight the natural colored products, a versatile color Palette is created with red, blues, pinks and oranges. The amount of text and images are decreased and important topics are highlighted through home page. Catalog Page: The Inconsistent cards are fixed in catalog page.
